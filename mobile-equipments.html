Power and Flexibility of Mobile Cranes

Mobile cranes play a vital role in modern construction and industrial operations, offering unmatched flexibility and lifting power. At UMAR SHAHZAD TRANSPORTS we operate a modern fleet of XCMG mobile cranes — available for rental in multiple lifting capacities including 25-ton, 100-ton, 160-ton, 200-ton, and 250-ton. These XCMG cranes are engineered to handle heavy loads safely and efficiently, making them essential for infrastructure, logistics, and large-scale projects. Their mobility and compact setup allow quick transportation and assembly at various job sites, improving project timelines and overall efficiency.

Key Benefits of Mobile Cranes

  • High mobility and easy transportation between sites
  • Quick setup and reduced project downtime
  • Cost-effective compared to fixed lifting systems
  • Increased efficiency in heavy lifting operations

Safety and Reliability

XCMG mobile cranes supplied by Umar Shahzad Transports are maintained to the highest safety standards and fitted with advanced safety mechanisms such as hydraulic stability systems, precision load indicators, and anti-sway controls. These features ensure smooth, controlled lifting operations while minimizing the risk of accidents or equipment damage. Regular inspections, preventative maintenance, and certified operator training further enhance safety, protecting both workers and assets on-site.

“XCMG cranes combine strength, precision, and safety — enabling heavy lifting operations that support construction, industry, and human progress.”

Helping Humans Work Smarter

Beyond their technical advantages, mobile cranes make work safer and easier for people. By removing the need for manual lifting of heavy loads and minimizing physical strain, XCMG cranes protect workers from injuries and fatigue. These machines allow teams to complete demanding tasks with greater control, speed, and accuracy, ultimately boosting productivity and workplace well-being.

  • Reduces manual labor and physical strain
  • Improves on-site safety for workers
  • Boosts project speed and accuracy
  • Supports sustainable and efficient operations

At UMAR SHAHZAD TRANSPORTS we pride ourselves on delivering reliable heavy lifting solutions with our fleet of XCMG cranes (25t → 250t). Whether your project requires a compact 25-ton crane or a heavy-lift 250-ton XCMG unit, our experienced operators and logistics team ensure each rental runs smoothly, safely, and on schedule.
